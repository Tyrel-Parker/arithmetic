🔢 Math Practice! 🌟

How to Play

Setting Up

  • Set the Maximum Number to control how hard the problems are. For example, max 10 keeps things easy; max 20 is harder.
  • Pick an operation: + add, − subtract, × multiply, ÷ divide.
  • For division, you can turn on Allow remainders to practice dividing with leftovers (e.g. 7 ÷ 2 = 3 R 1).
  • Turn on Allow ± to include negative numbers.

Choosing a Mode

  • ⏱️ Timed — Answer a set number of questions, then see your score.
  • ♾️ Infinite — Keep going as long as you want. Press Stop when you're done.
  • 📊 Running Average — Same as Infinite, but your average time per question is tracked.

Answering Questions

  • Use the number pad on screen to type your answer, then press ✓.
  • You have 6 seconds to answer each question — watch the countdown!
  • If the timer runs out, the correct answer is shown. Press ✓ to move on.
  • Wrong answers show ✗ — keep trying until you get it or time runs out.

Your Score

At the end you'll see how many you got right out of the total, like 8/10 - 80%. Try to beat your best!

Other Buttons

  • 🎨 — Change the color theme.
  • 🏠 Home — Go back to the main screen at any time.
